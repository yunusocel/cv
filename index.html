About me

I work as a Software Test Engineer at Software Test Directorate of Aselsan A.Ş. which is the defense corporation in top 50 companies in the world. I am really appreciate to be a member of the Software Test Team at Aselsan. Also, i have worked before with the same position at R&D department of White Goods Design Verification Directorate of Vestel Elektronik A.Ş. Vestel Elektronik is the place which was the beginning of my career path and it is one of 13 companies awarded with "TPM World Class". I owe my career i have today to my past experiences, my dreams and childhood. That's why i would like to start my words by saying thank both Vestel and Aselsan.

I have been interested in art and technology since my childhood. In addition to painting my dreams by pushing the limits of my creativity, the ever-evolving technology brought by the software has always excited me. In the end of my high school years, I chose Electrical & Electronics Engineering department at Ege University by achieving 19.382 ranking degree in the university exam in order to be a producing part of developing technology and have graduated with 3.04/4 gpa by keeping the same interest and enthusiasm. During my education, I encouraged myself to research, question and learn in every field by taking part in many different projects in addition to the electricity courses i chose in my last year. Among the projects i have been involved in, the most important project is my graduation project named Android Based Virtual Simulative Assistant for Electric Vehicle Buyers Mobile App: myBestEV. I got awarded with Superior Success Certificate for Graduation Project. with this project by Ege University. I have not published this app because of copyrights but i will after doing some changes on the app.

During my education, i always tried to improve myself not only technically but also socially. In this context, I have attended various seminars, events and theater courses organized by the student societies at Ege University. I participated in Erasmus+ Youth Exchange Projects in UK and Spain with the participation of more than one students that come from different countries. I have studied at Electronics Engineering department of the University of West Attica (UNIWA) with Erasmus in order to increase my knowledge and experience, and to get to know different cultures closer.

I would like to put an end to my words by saying every training i had, every activity, seminar, course and project i have attended and every person i have met is an experience for me. With this experience in my pocket, i am coming to realize my dreams that i painted from my childhood. I hope we can paint our common dreams together one day.
